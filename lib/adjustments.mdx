## Module: `lib/adjustments.py`

Handles the two main pre-valuation adjustments that Damodaran recommends.

## Key functions

### `capitalize_rd()`

Capitalizes R&D expenses into a research asset over a specified amortization period. Returns adjusted EBIT, research asset value, and adjusted invested capital.

### `convert_leases()`

Converts operating lease commitments into a debt equivalent using the pre-tax cost of debt as the discount rate. Returns the lease debt, adjusted EBIT, and adjusted total debt.
