## What it does

Collects and validates the four assumptions that drive the 10-year FCFF projection. For each assumption, the skill presents historical data, industry benchmarks, and distribution statistics to help calibrate.

## The four assumptions

| Assumption | Maps to | What it controls |
|------------|---------|-----------------|
| Revenue growth rate | Input sheet row 26 | Top-line trajectory during high-growth period |
| Target operating margin | Input sheet row 28 | Where EBIT margin converges by the target year |
| Margin convergence year | Input sheet row 29 | How quickly margins transition to the target |
| Sales-to-capital ratio | Input sheet row 32 | Reinvestment efficiency — revenue per dollar of invested capital |

## How assumptions interact

These four numbers together determine every line in the 10-year projection:

- **Revenue** = prior year × (1 + growth rate), declining linearly toward stable growth
- **EBIT** = revenue × operating margin (converging from current to target)
- **Reinvestment** = revenue change / sales-to-capital ratio
- **FCFF** = EBIT × (1 − tax rate) − reinvestment
